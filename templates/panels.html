The goal of The Center is to research existing applications of Sympathetic Intelligence and then use that research to find ways to optimise those existing applications.

Because of the ubiquitous nature of Sympathetice Intelligence, we have narrowed our focus to three distinct professions in the community where the application of sympathetic intelligence is critical.   • The Healthcare community, and;
